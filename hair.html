Hair tips!

  1. Pick a hairstyle that you would like to go well with your dress. Ask family and friends for suggestions or look online for inspiration if you're stumped!
  2. Updo? Half up half down? Down?
  3. Pick the place you want to get your hair done
  4. Make an appointment for a time that's suitable for you and your hair stylist
  5. Go to your appointment and get your hair done!
  6. You're all done!
