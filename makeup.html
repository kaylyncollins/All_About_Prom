Makeup tips

  • Pick a place where you want to get your makeup done, pick a friend to do it, or decide if you will do it yourself
  • Make an appointment unless you are doing it yourself
  • Go to the person you are getting it done from and talk to them about what you would like for your special day
  • Go on the day to get it done and be sure it's what you want
  • You're all done!
